Resolve the Satisfaction Crisis

Deploy post-purchase surveys and route all scores 1–2 into a 24-hour personal recovery workflow. Set a KPI target: move 25% of low-scorers to score 3+ within 90 days. No other retention investment will succeed while the underlying experience is broken.

Fix New Customer Onboarding (98% churn)

Build a structured 30-60-90 day email journey for all new accounts. Trigger a 10–15% second-purchase incentive at day 28. Track time-to-second-purchase as the leading retention KPI — if customers don't buy again within 60 days, they almost never will.

High Impact
3

Accelerate Bronze → Silver Transitions

Surface real-time spend-to-next-tier progress in account dashboards and transactional emails. Run a bonus points event for Bronze customers within $50 of the $400 Silver threshold. Bronze churns at 88% vs Silver at 71% — the transition dramatically changes the retention profile.

Medium Impact
4

Deploy Churn Model in Production

Score all active customers weekly using the Gradient Boosting model. Automatically route customers with churn probability > 0.70 into a high-priority retention flow. A/B test interventions vs. control groups to measure model lift and continuously improve targeting.
